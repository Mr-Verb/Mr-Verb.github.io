Criminal Luck

Challenge Description:

The advanced, city-wide surveillance system, designed to target and intercept vehicles based on precise coordinates, has been hacked! Several criminals have exploited this breach and escaped into the 1,000 by 1,000 grid of city blocks. Their movements are erratic, and tracking them has become a daunting task for the authorities. Your mission is to analyze their unpredictable escape routes, pinpoint their final locations, and help the city regain control of its streets. Can you outsmart their luck and bring these criminals to justice before they vanish within the sprawling urban grid? Flag Format:FLAG{this_is_your_flag} Connect to Challenge: nc 7878

Initial Thoughts

  • Investigate the endpoint provided (after starting the lab).
    • How do I connect to the challenge?
    • Is port 7878 the only port I need?
    • Are there other mechanisms other than nc that I can use?
  • What are the inputs?
    • What inputs do I need to provide (and are they different each time)?
  • Understand the ouput data
    • What are the challenge outputs (are they different each time)?
    • What output format is used (string, json object, random garbage, etc)?
